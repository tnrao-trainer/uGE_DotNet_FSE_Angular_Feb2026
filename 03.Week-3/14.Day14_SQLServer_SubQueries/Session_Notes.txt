Working with Sub queries
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

--  Correlated Sub Queries 
SELECT Ename, Salary, Deptno
FROM Emp E1
WHERE Salary >
      (SELECT AVG(Salary)
       FROM Emp E2
       WHERE E2.Deptno = E1.Deptno);

How it Works:	
a.  The outer query selects each employee.
b. The inner query calculates the average salary of that employee's department.
c. The employee's salary is compared with that department average.
d. If the salary is higher, the employee is displayed.




		
	Find employees who earn the highest salary in their department.	
		
		
		SELECT Ename, Salary, Deptno
FROM Emp E1
WHERE Salary =
      (SELECT MAX(Salary)
       FROM Emp E2
       WHERE E2.Deptno = E1.Deptno);
