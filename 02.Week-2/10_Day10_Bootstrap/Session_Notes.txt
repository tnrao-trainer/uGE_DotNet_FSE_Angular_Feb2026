Pagination  in Bootstrap
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

-->  Pagination is the process of dividing large sets of digital content or search results into smaller, discrete, and numbered pages. It enhances user experience by reducing cognitive load, improves website performance by loading fewer items at once, and aids SEO by allowing search engines to index extensive content more efficiently.
