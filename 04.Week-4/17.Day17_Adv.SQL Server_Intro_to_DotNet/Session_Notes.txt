ACID properties
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
ACID properties in databases—Atomicity, Consistency, Isolation, and Durability—guarantee reliable transaction processing, ensuring data integrity even during failures. 

These properties ensure transactions are 
	---	all-or-nothing (Atomicity), 
	---  valid (Consistency), 
	---  independent (Isolation), 
	---  permanent (Durability). 
	
They are crucial for maintaining consistent, error-free database states.
